About SonicGliders

-Unearth the secrets of an ever-shifting Sonic while trying desperately not to die. An atmospheric open-world rougelike. Explore an ancient, ruined Sonic with nothing but a sword and the clothes on your back (and maybe a bunch of volatile and destructive bows). Enjoy the lack of obstructions on your way, except about a trillion territorial cycloptic mages who will repeatedly pepper you with various explosive spells. Socialize with a cacophony of incredibly joyous and friendly characters, none of whom posses crippling depression from living in a world that is falling apart at the seams Revel in the stupendous, incredible, tantalizing, vast, incontrovertible, and dynamic art direction (this sentence is sponsored by our art director).
